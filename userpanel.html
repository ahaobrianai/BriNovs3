🔑 API Key — Deriv Token
Your token is stored only on this device and never sent to our servers. It is sent directly to Deriv's API over a secure WebSocket connection.
How to get your API token
1 Go to app.deriv.com/account/api-token and log in to your Deriv account.
2 Click Create new token. Give it a name (e.g. "BriNovs Engine").
3 Select the scopes you need: Read (for balance and account info) and Trade (for the Auto Bot to place trades on your behalf).
4 Copy the generated token and paste it in the field above, then click Connect.
5 Keep your token private. Do not share it. You can revoke it at any time from your Deriv account settings.
